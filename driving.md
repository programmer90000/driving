# How to enter the car
1. Push the seat forward or backwards until I am in a good position
2. Fasten the seatbelt
3. Make sure the gearstick is in neutral
5. Make sure the handbrake is up
4. Adjust the mirrors. I should be able to see almost all of the back window from the main mirror. I should be able to see the car handles and the road from the side mirrors

# How to start the car
1. Look in all of the mirrors and blind spot
2. Twist the key to start the engine
3. Select the turn signal
4. Press the clutch pedal fully
5. While holding the clutch pedal, move the gearstick fully left and then up for the first gear. Keep my foot fully planted on the clutch pedal
6. Look in all of the mirrors to ensure no cars are coming. Turn my head right to look in the blind spot
7. Lift my left foot slightly, until the car reaches biting point
8. Push the brake pedal down and then push the handbrake down
9. Let go of the brake pedal and turn the car. The car should begin moving slowly
10. Begin to press the accelerator and let go of the clutch
11. Steer smoothly to enter the lane or the flow of traffic
16. Cancel the turn signal
