# Safety and Your Vehicle - Driving Theory Notes

## 1. Basic Safety Checks
- **Tyres**: Check condition, tread depth, and pressure regularly
- **Tread depth**: Legal minimum is 1.6mm across central Â¾ of tyre and around entire circumference
- **Lights**: All lights must be clean and working (headlights, brake lights, indicators)
- **Oil level**: Check regularly using dipstick
- **Brakes**: Check they work correctly before setting off

## 2. Before Driving
- **Windows and mirrors**: Clean all for clear vision
- **Number plates**: Must be clean and legible
- **Seat adjustment**: Ensure you can reach all controls
- **Seat belts**: All passengers must wear them properly
- **Head restraint**: Adjust to correct position (top inline with eyes/ears)

## 3. Load Security
- **Secure all loads** to prevent movement while driving
- **Heavy items**: Place low down over the rear axle (boot) to maintain vehicle balance and stability
- **Don't overload** - check vehicle's maximum weight limit
- **Loose items**: Can cause injury in a collision

## 4. Environmental Considerations
- **Avoid wasting fuel**: Smooth acceleration, proper gear use, correct tyre pressure
- **Reduce pollution**: Regular servicing maintains efficient engine operation
- **Noise pollution**: Don't rev engine unnecessarily
- **Disposal**: Dispose of old oil and batteries at recycling centres

## 5. Vehicle Security
- **Lock vehicle** whenever leaving it unattended
- **Remove valuables** from view or take them with you
- **Park in well-lit areas** where possible

## 6. Key Legal Requirements
- **Valid insurance** - must have at least third party cover
- **Valid MOT** - required for vehicles over 3 years old
- **Valid road tax** - must be displayed/registered correctly
- **Driving licence** - must be appropriate for vehicle type
