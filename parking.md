# Parking on a straight road
When I am parked on a straight road, keep the wheel straight. If the car moves, it will stay still

# Parking on a downhill road
When I am parked downhill, move the wheel towards the street. If the car moves, it will go to the street instead of the road

# Parking on an uphill road
When I am parked uphill, move the wheel towards the road. If the car moves, it will reverse to the street instead of the road
